About NSG Health

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services to individuals and families across the country through our innovative mobile clinic model.

Our Vision

To revolutionize healthcare delivery by eliminating geographical barriers and ensuring everyone has access to medical services when they need them.

Our Founder

Dr. Geofrey Mose

With over 15 years of experience in the medical field, Dr. Mose founded NSG Health to address the healthcare accessibility challenges faced by many in our communities. His vision of a mobile clinic that brings services directly to patients has transformed healthcare delivery for thousands.
